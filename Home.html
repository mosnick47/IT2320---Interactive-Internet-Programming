About the HAKA

To someone who doesn't know, this may seem like a silly bunch of screaming and funny faces. But to the Maori, the indigenous people of New Zealond, this was their war dance. They grunted and made screams and faces with bulging eyes and poking their tounges out to intimidate their opponents and to boost their own morale.
